About Me

Who am I?

I'd describe myself as an aspiring data scientist. Currently a master degree big data student at Warsaw School of Economics. Presently I'm engaged about predictive modeling with SAS and Python. I'm also on the way to certificate „Data Scientist with SAS system”.

My background

I must mention about my background because it's unique I believe. Although I am studying master degree, I am currently a Master of Science in Engineering. I have 3 years background as a structural designer. My work is about using softwares to design buildings in Sweden remotely from Warsaw. I collaborate in buildings design processes which are envolving many people and even factories. We use BIM(building information modelling) to improve buildings processes. It is like CL/CD processes in IT. In spite of it is not an IT related job, but it allowed me to develop on many levels and gave me a lot of satisfaction. I had to communicate with project leaders in Sweden remotely from Warsaw with tight design schedule. It was absorbing and time-consuming job where I could really expand my soft skills, self-organization and english level. It also gave me some basics in working with different files like xml, csv, files pipelines, windows configurations, bug reporting just like Jira is doing in IT, files share as we enter cloud environment together with designers from Sweden (btw. that is what leaded me to big data studies).

Homever big data technologies consumed me completely. I had mathematics background from engineer studies and coding has become a curiosity for me. I was always really into computers and now I don't see myself anywhere else (I'm enjoying coding and using new tools, even when creating this page). At present time I think I'm ready and able to change and start new IT related job as a junior data scientist/engineer. Give me a try!

Why I think my background is worth mention ? Data Science is not only about programming. It takes a lot of skills to be a valued specialist.
