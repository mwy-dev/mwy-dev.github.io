My story

I must mention about my background because it is unique I believe. Although I am studying master degree, I am already a Master of Science in Civil Engineering.

I have 3 years background as a structural designer. My work is about using softwares to design buildings in Sweden remotely from Warsaw. I collaborate in buildings design processes which are envolving many people and even factories. We are using BIM(building information modelling) to improve buildings processes. It is similar to CL/CD processes.

In spite of it is not an IT related job, but it has allowed me to develop on many levels and gave me a lot of satisfaction. I had to cooperate with project leaders in Sweden with tight design schedule.

It was absorbing and time-consuming job where I could really expand my soft skills, self-organization and english level. It also gave me some basics in working with different files like xml, csv, files pipelines, windows configurations, bug reporting in tool similar to Jira, files share from user side when we entered cloud environment together with designers from Sweden.

That is what leaded me to went for Big Data studies.

Homever big data technologies consumed me completely. I have math and problem solving background from earlier studies and my work as a structural engineer. Now I am interested in the latest technologies and programming. I was always into computers and now I do not see myself anywhere else. I am enjoying using new tools, even when creating this github based page(first time in my life).

At present time I think I'm able to start new career as a junior data scientist.
